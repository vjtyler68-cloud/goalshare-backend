Privacy Policy

GoalShare · Last updated: July 12, 2026

GoalShare (“the app,” “we,” “us”) helps you set goals, build daily habits, and track your progress. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep it plain-English on purpose.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash) when you create an account.
  • Content you create — goals, tasks, “My Why” entries, affirmations, gratitude journal entries, budget data, nutrition logs, leads/contacts you add, and profile details. This is your data; we store it so it syncs to your account or stays on your device.
  • Photos — if you choose to add a profile photo or attach a photo to a lead, we access only the images you pick. Lead photos are stored on your device.
  • Health & activity data (optional) — if you enable Apple Health sync, we read activity data (steps and calories burned) from Apple Health with your explicit permission, solely to show your exercise progress in the app. We never sell or share health data, and we do not use it for advertising.
  • Purchase information — subscriptions are processed by Apple. We receive subscription status (active/expired), never your payment details.
  • Device basics — standard technical information needed to run the service (such as app version and error logs).

2. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not show third-party ads or share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not use your health data for anything other than displaying it to you.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the app: sync your goals and content, show your progress, send the reminders you set.
  • To secure accounts: verification codes, password resets.
  • To improve the app: fixing crashes and errors.

4. Where your data lives

Much of your data (budget, nutrition logs, daily tasks, leads, streaks, Bible highlights) is stored locally on your device. Account data and synced content are stored on our servers (hosted in the United States). Images you upload to your profile may be stored with our cloud storage providers.

5. Notifications

Reminders (daily tasks, streaks, lead follow-ups) are scheduled locally on your device and only if you grant notification permission. You can turn them off anytime in iOS Settings.

6. Deleting your data

You can delete your account directly in the app (Profile → Delete Account). This permanently removes your account and associated server-side data. Locally stored data is removed when you delete the app. You may also email us (below) to request deletion.

7. Children

GoalShare is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g., California, EU/EEA), you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information. Contact us and we will honor these requests.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

10. Contact

Questions or requests: support@goalsharewin.com
